NASA RockOn!

The team receives a sensor kit from NASA and uses it to assemble a payload that will be launched into space aboard a sounding rocket. The system includes humidity sensor, accelerometer, Geiger counter, gyroscope, pressure meter.

The team had to put the system through rigorous testing and verification to ensure reliability during flight.

Members include Cuong Nguyen, Ellie Rosentel, Mergim Berisha, Gerard Piccini.
